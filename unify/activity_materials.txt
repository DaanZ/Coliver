Camping equipment, tents, sleeping bags, air mattresses, groundsheets, hammocks, diving masks, tennis equipment, beach tennis, court, rackets, balls are at your disposal. You can borrow them as you wish in exchange for good care. Please put them back in their place once you have used them, put them away in the boxes and check that they are in good condition. Please also machine clean the sleeping bags before storing them. Please report any missing or damaged items. The goal is for everyone to be able to enjoy their equipment in the best possible way.
